About Us

Hi, I'm Joshua McKenzie, the founder of Joshua's Photography. Photography isn’t just my profession—it’s my passion. I believe every moment tells a story, and my goal is to capture those stories in a way that feels authentic, timeless, and true to you.

Whether it’s a wedding, portrait session, event, or a quiet candid moment, I approach each shoot with creativity, care, and a commitment to quality. I strive to create images that feel as real and unforgettable as the moment itself.

Based in Jamaica, I work with clients near and far. I’d love the chance to connect and create something beautiful together.
